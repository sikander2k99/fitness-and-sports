Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the event?

Please bring water, a towel, and comfortable workout clothes. For outdoor events, sunscreen and a mat for yoga/meditation are recommended.

Can beginners join the events?

Yes! Our events are open to all fitness levels. Our trainers will offer modifications to accommodate different experience levels.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your registration up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. After that, cancellations are non-refundable.
